    • Taurus (April 20–May 20)

    Taurus is an earth sign represented by the bull. Like their celestial spirit animal, Taureans enjoy relaxing in serene, bucolic environments surrounded by soft sounds, soothing aromas, and succulent flavors.

    Positive Traits
    The people born under this sign have immense energy. They are very generous and down to earth. They are always ready to help people. They prefer independence and are very persistent in their efforts, and hence very less likely to experience any setbacks or failures. They are very dependable and patient.

    Negative Traits
    Taureans are very possessive, which can make them very resentful and jealous. If they do not feel motivated, they are very laid-back and lazy. They tend to be self-centered and materialistic, because of their overly ambitious attitude.

    Virgo (August 23–September 22)

    This post is created by....

    Virgo is an earth sign historically represented by the goddess of wheat and agriculture, an association that speaks to Virgo’s deep-rooted presence in the material world. Virgos are logical, practical, and systematic in their approach to life. This earth sign is a perfectionist at heart and isn’t afraid to improve skills through diligent and consistent practice.

    Positive Traits
    Virgos are intelligent people, and they are always eager to learn more. They are very practical beings and do not believe in whims or fancies. The Virgins are very watchful, cautious and analytical in their approach. They are reliable and trustworthy friends.

    Negative Traits
    Virgos are very critical of everything and everyone. They are very conservative and like to do things in old-fashioned ways. They are extremely fastidious and fussy. Virgos do not like sugar coating their words and thus can get too harsh sometimes.

    Capricorn (December 22–January 19)

    This post is created by....

    The last earth sign of the zodiac, Capricorn is represented by the sea goat, a mythological creature with the body of a goat and tail of a fish. Accordingly, Capricorns are skilled at navigating both the material and emotional realms.

    Positive Traits
    Highly ambitious and driven, the Capricorns have big goals in life. They take time in making their decisions and do not believe in haste. They are very practical and disciplined in their approach towards life. They are loyal and true friends.

    Negative Traits
    They are friendly, but they like to be in the company of a few selected people as they tend to get too shy. They can get pessimistic and negative. They seldom change their opinions or views.
