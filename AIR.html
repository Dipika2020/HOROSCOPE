    • Gemini (May 21–June 21)

    This post is created by....

    Have you ever been so busy that you wished you could clone yourself just to get everything done? That’s the Gemini experience in a nutshell. Appropriately symbolized by the celestial twins, this air sign was interested in so many pursuits that it had to double itself.

    Positive Traits
    They are extremely knowledgeable and intelligent. Geminis are filled with oodles of enthusiasm. This zodiac sign’s funny characteristics include their sharp wit and sense of humour. There is not a dull moment if you are in their company.

    Negative Traits
    The people born under this sign are very restless and anxious. They feel lost because of their lack of consistency and decision-making ability.

    Libra (September 23–October 23)

    This post is created by....

    Libra is an air sign represented by the scales (interestingly, the only inanimate object of the zodiac), an association that reflects Libra's fixation on balance and harmony. Libra is obsessed with symmetry and strives to create equilibrium in all areas of life.

    Positive Traits
    Librans are the fairest of all and being just is one of their highest valued virtues. Governed by Venus, Librans are very loving and romantic souls. They are very tactful and know how to get things done by people. They are diplomatic but very charming people.

    Negative Traits
    Decision-making can be a tedious task for the Librans. They are so indecisive that it may get difficult to trust them. They are superficial and lay more emphasis on outer beauty than inner beauty.

    Aquarius (January 20–February 18)

    This post is created by....

    Despite the “aqua” in its name, Aquarius is actually the last air sign of the zodiac. Aquarius is represented by the water bearer, the mystical healer who bestows water, or life, upon the land. Accordingly, Aquarius is the most humanitarian astrological sign.

    Positive Traits
    Aquarians are very creative and intelligent humans. They are very friendly and generally have a large circle of friends. Once committed in any relationship, Aquarians will remain faithful till the end. They like to be financially and emotionally independent.

    Negative Traits
    They are extremists, and it is either this way or that way; no middle way. They sometimes become distant or aloof for no reason. They are very inconsistent and unpredictable. They may listen to people but will do what they think is right and can get extremely stubborn.

    Water Signs Will Swim Away in a River of Tears

    water signs—Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces—are known for being sensitive and sentimental. They tend to hold on to people and items long past their expiration dates, and their emotions are always flowing like the waves of the oceans. These signs have intense dreams, visions, and intuition so strong that it’s basically a psychic power.

    All About Air Signs, the Zodiac’s Social Smarties

    Air signs that’s Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius are the thinkers, communicators, and doers of the zodiac.They breeze through life, never stopping to catch their breath. They have a “live and let live” mentality, and their intelligence helps them make decisions easily.

    Fighting With a Fire Sign? Yeah, You’re Gonna Lose

    There are three fire signs in the zodiac- Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius. All three are aggressive, lusty, and impassioned. Never apologetic, they use their drive to make sh*t happen. They believe they were born for greatness—and TBH, they’re not wrong. In fact, they have the utmost drive for success…..
